Why the tittle
Between Worlds

Trevor Noah's experiences reflect his mixed-race heritage in apartheid South Africa,
where he often felt caught between different cultural identities.
The title emphasizes the struggle of navigating multiple
worlds;- black and white, African and Western.

Quote

"Abel wanted a traditional marriage with a
traditional wife… but he never fell in love with
subservient women… 'He only wants a woman
who is free because his dream is to put her
in a cage.'”

How this quote is depicted
in current society

What they believe

Fathers in many families sees themselves as the head of the households
and their wives are expected to be subservient;-
whereby they cannot make any decision.

Defiance

Patricia, Abel's wife, was strong and independent.
They are some women who are resourceful and reilient.They are fiercely
independent and takes charge of their life despite the societal
constraints placed upon them. A good example is my sponsor; Linda Kamau
she is one of the great women I know that are fiercely independent
and strongly focuses on closing the gap of women in tech world by sponsoring
young ladies to learn technology.

More so, currently most women values education and strives for better
future of themselves and their children.
This emphasizes equality and breaking away
from traditional expectations of women.
